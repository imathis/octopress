2006年8月14日，Google推出了Blogger in beta，增加了以下特性（摘自http://buzz.blogger.com/2006/08/blogger-in-beta.html）：

  • Categorize your posts with labels
  • Control who can read your blog
  • Change the appearance and content of your blog with your mouse instead of HTML

（不翻译了）

总之，它对Blog的所有者和浏览者都更加友好，再加上我喜欢尝尝鲜，所以我就搬过来了。
大家可能关心以前的blog，官方给出的解释是：
As we release this version, we're limiting the number of people who can switch over. (You'll see a link on your Blogger dashboard when you are able to move.) Eventually, of course, everyone will be able to transition their blogs to the new version. Thanks in advance for your patience as we roll it out.If you can't wait, you can create a new account on the beta now and make a new blog to test out the new features. Because the new Blogger uses Google Accounts, you can use your existing Google login, or create a new one. (Later, you'll be able to merge your current and beta accounts, and have all your blogs in one place.)

我的blog的控制台上没有出现升级的信息，所以就只有用我的Google帐户来新建一个blog来使用Blogger in beta，以后可以合并以前的blog和Blogger in beta。
原有的blog上作了重定向，新的blog的URL是http://kzy-prism-beta.blogspot.com/，Atom和RSS放在侧边栏上了。
谢谢大家支持！

PS：浏览原有blog的方法：打开http://kzy-prism.blogspot.com/，页面加载基本完成后，迅速按下Esc键或按工具栏上的停止按钮。
